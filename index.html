Women's Safety in public spaces
ECHOGUARD
Echoguard is a real-time threat detection system aiming to create safer environments for women in public spaces. Leveraging advanced analytics and surveillance, the system detects anomalies, tracks gender distribution, and identifies potential threats, such as lone women at night or suspicious gestures. It generates alerts for law enforcement to intervene promptly, preventing crimes against women. By analyzing historical data, the system also identifies high-risk areas, enabling strategic planning for city safety. This proactive approach fosters a secure atmosphere, empowering women to feel safer in public spaces.

Echoguard's Lone Women Detection feature uses advanced analytics and real-time surveillance to identify women walking alone in isolated areas, particularly at night. The system alerts authorities and nearby security personnel, enabling swift intervention and ensuring the woman's safety. This feature addresses the vulnerability of women in solitary situations, providing an added layer of protection and reassurance. By detecting and responding to potential threats, Echoguard's Lone Women Detection feature helps prevent crimes and fosters a safer environment for women to move freely and confidently.

Echoguard's SOS Gesture Recognition feature uses AI-powered video analytics to detect distress signals from women in real-time. The system identifies specific hand gestures or body language indicating distress, such as waving arms or tapping feet. Upon detection, Echoguard immediately alerts nearby security personnel and authorities, ensuring prompt assistance. This feature empowers women to discreetly signal for help in potentially threatening situations, providing a vital lifeline and enhancing their safety. By recognizing SOS gestures, Echoguard offers a proactive and intuitive solution for women's safety.
